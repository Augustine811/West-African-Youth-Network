Our Major Partners

We are proud to collaborate with these distinguished organizations from around the world, working together to create positive change and promote peace, democracy, and human dignity.

AGIAMONDO

Civil Peace Service Network, Germany

AGIAMONDO is part of the German Civil Peace Service network, focusing on peacebuilding and conflict transformation worldwide. They support local partners in conflict regions with qualified peace experts.

National Democratic Institute

United States of America

NDI is a nonprofit, nonpartisan organization working to support and strengthen democratic institutions worldwide through citizen participation, openness, and accountability in government.

Karibu Foundation

Africa, Kenya

The Karibu Foundation supports alternative voices and initiatives from the Global South that challenge dominant paradigms and work towards social, economic, and ecological justice.

DIGNITY

Danish Institute Against Torture, Denmark, Europe

DIGNITY works to prevent torture, violence, and other inhuman treatment while promoting human rights and rehabilitating victims of torture through research, documentation, and treatment.
