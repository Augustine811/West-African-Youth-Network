Youth Program Coordinator

WAYN Sierra Leone

Job Description

We are seeking a dynamic and experienced Youth Program Coordinator to lead our community engagement initiatives. The ideal candidate will have a passion for youth development and community empowerment, with proven experience in program management and stakeholder engagement.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement youth programs and activities
  • Coordinate with community partners and stakeholders
  • Monitor program outcomes and prepare reports
  • Mentor and support youth participants
  • Organize workshops, training sessions, and events
  • Manage program budget and resources

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Social Sciences, Education, or related field
  • 3+ years of experience in youth programming or community development
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work with diverse groups of young people
  • Strong organizational and project management skills
  • Knowledge of the Sierra Leone youth context is an advantage
