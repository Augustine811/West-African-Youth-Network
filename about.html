About West African Youth Network

Empowering Youth for Social Transformation Since 2001

Who We Are

The West African Youth Network (WAYN) is a youth-led organization established in 2001 with a focus on empowering young people across Sierra Leone and West Africa. For over two decades, we have been at the forefront of youth development, advocating for meaningful youth participation in governance and peacebuilding processes.

Our organization works directly with marginalized youth, providing them with the knowledge, skills, and platforms needed to actively contribute to social transformation in their communities.

👁️

WAYN Vision

WAYN envisions a society in which youth development is prioritized within the socio-political discourse, whereby young people drive social transformation by participating in governance processes, advocating for youth issues, and assuming leadership roles in peacebuilding endeavors.

We aspire to enhance the capacity of young people by providing safe spaces for youth in Sierra Leone to develop their potential and contribute meaningfully to national development.

🎯

WAYN Mission

Our mission is to empower and capacitate the next generation by fostering youth participation in governance, promoting youth development, and providing safe spaces for growth, expression, and learning.

Through our initiatives, we drive social transformation, foster peace-building efforts, and amplify advocacy for inclusive policies that address the needs and aspirations of young people.

We are committed to creating an environment that nurtures leadership, embraces diversity, and inspires positive change in Sierra Leone.

⚖️

Core Mandate

WAYN works with marginalized young people to teach them about the existing political and legal systems in the country and respective communities. This is done by empowering them to raise their voices and stand up for their rights and representation in society.

The organization addresses critical issues including conflict resolution and corruption in the educational system through community and college engagements, offering training courses and innovative methods, such as Participatory Video.

🤝
