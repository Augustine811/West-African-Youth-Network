Core Mandate

WAYN works with marginalized young people to teach them about the existing political and legal systems in the country and respective communities. This is done by empowering them to raise their voices and stand up for their rights and representation in society.

The organization is working on the topics of conflict resolution and Corruption in the Educational system as well as issues of Conflict resolution through community and college engagements, offering training courses and innovative methods, such as Participatory Video.
