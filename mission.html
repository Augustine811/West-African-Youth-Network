A Message from WAYN

At the West African Youth Network, our mission is deeply rooted in the belief that young people are not just the leaders of tomorrow, but the changemakers of today. For over two decades, we have dedicated ourselves to creating platforms where youth voices are heard, valued, and empowered to drive meaningful social transformation.

Through strategic partnerships, capacity building, and advocacy, we continue to break barriers and create opportunities for young people to participate actively in governance and peacebuilding processes. Our commitment remains unwavering - to build a Sierra Leone where every young person can realize their full potential and contribute to national development.

Together for Youth Empowerment,
The WAYN Team

WAYN Mission

Our mission is to empower and capacitate the next generation by fostering youth participation in governance, promoting youth development, and providing safe spaces for growth, expression, and learning.

Through our initiatives, we drive social transformation, foster peace-building efforts, and amplify advocacy for inclusive policies that address the needs and aspirations of young people. We are committed to creating an environment that nurtures leadership, embraces diversity, and inspires positive change in Sierra Leone.
